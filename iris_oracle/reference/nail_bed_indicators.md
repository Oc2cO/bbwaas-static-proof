# Nail Bed Indicators (Onychomancy and Modern Insights)

## Overview

Onychomancy is an ancient form of divination that interprets the shape, texture and appearance of fingernails.  Practitioners sometimes polish the nails with oil and read reflections or symbols, connecting them to reflexology points, chakras, astrological planets or even tarot cards【582153179858106†L124-L172】.  Each finger is associated with a planet – thumb (Mars), index finger (Jupiter), middle finger (Saturn), ring finger (Apollo/Sun) and little finger (Mercury)【582153179858106†L149-L186】.  Long nails are believed to enhance intuition by enlarging the “crown” and “third eye” chakras【582153179858106†L149-L186】.  While these interpretations lack scientific backing, they offer metaphorical insights.

From a health perspective, modern medicine notes that changes in nail colour, texture and the visibility of the lunula (the half‑moon at the base of the nail) can sometimes indicate systemic issues.  For example, red lunulae have been associated with cirrhosis or heart failure, blue with Wilson’s disease or diabetes, brown with chronic kidney disease, and white with certain liver or kidney conditions【827231065116109†L61-L76】【827231065116109†L100-L133】.  However, physicians caution that nail changes alone should not be used for diagnosis; consult a healthcare provider for concerns【827231065116109†L138-L144】.

## Traditional Interpretations

- **Shape:**  Rounded or oval nails are thought to reflect sociability and adaptability; square nails suggest practicality and earthiness; almond or pointed nails signify creativity and sensitivity.  Practitioners of “kikimancy” link nail shapes to the four classical elements (earth, air, fire, water) and corresponding personality types【582153179858106†L124-L172】.
- **Colour:**  Healthy nails are usually pale pink.  In onychomancy, pale nails may suggest tranquillity; red nails indicate passion; white nails are associated with purity; yellow or green hues are seen as signs of imbalance.  Medically, yellow nails can occur with tetracycline therapy or yellow nail syndrome【827231065116109†L61-L76】.
- **Texture:**  Smooth nails imply steady energy.  Vertical ridges might be read as a life structured by routine; horizontal ridges (Beau’s lines) could signify past stress.  Peeling or brittle nails may symbolise fragmentation in one’s life.
- **Moons (lunulae):**  In onychomancy, the size and visibility of lunulae correlate with vitality.  Large, clear moons suggest strong energy; small or absent moons may indicate depleted vitality.  In medical literature, variations in lunula size can reflect anaemia or malnutrition, but differences are often normal【827231065116109†L61-L76】.
- **Finger associations:**  Each finger’s nail is linked to a planet:  
  - **Thumb (Mars):** Courage, action and drive.  Nail markings here are interpreted as signals about personal will.  
  - **Index (Jupiter):** Leadership and ambition.  
  - **Middle (Saturn):** Responsibility and discipline.  
  - **Ring (Apollo/Sun):** Creativity, joy and recognition.  
  - **Little (Mercury):** Communication and intellect【582153179858106†L149-L186】.
